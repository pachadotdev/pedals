Maple Booster (revision 2)

This effect is the result of multiple iterations until I found out an affordable approximation to the Dallas Rangemaster (TM) pedal without compromising sound quality.

Sir Dr. Brian May used a Dallas Rangemaster (TM) it to record the first Queen album in 1972.

This pedal features true bypass, a volume control, and a 2 positions switch to select between more high or medium frequencies.

Please send me a message indicating your colour preference. I can cut acrylic in any colour from the second image.

This pedal comes with a sixpence coin.

All my pedals are handmade in Canada, one by one, as I get orders. The stock reflects the input parts I have readily available to start building a pedal as soon as it's ordered.
